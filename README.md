Decision Trees and Random forest
For this project, we will explore publicly available data from LendingClub.com. Lending Club connects borrowers, who need money, with investors, who have money. As an investor, you likely want to invest in people who show a strong chance of paying you back. We will create a model to help predict this. Lending Club had an interesting year in 2016, so let's examine some of their data while keeping that context in mind. This data is from before they went public. We will use lending data from 2007 to 2010 and try to classify and predict whether a borrower repaid their loan in full. The CSV file provided on Blackboard has already been cleaned of NA values. Here is what each column represents: - **credit.policy**: 1 if the customer meets the credit criteria of LendingClub.com, and 0 otherwise. - **purpose**: The reason for the loan. It can be "credit_card," "debt_consolidation," "educational," "major_purchase," "small_business," or "all_other." - **int.rate**: The loan's interest rate as a proportion (for example, an 11% rate is stored as 0.11). Borrowers deemed more risky by LendingClub.com receive higher interest rates. - **installment**: The monthly payments owed by the borrower if the loan is funded. - **log.annual.inc**: The natural log of the borrower's self-reported annual income. - **dti**: The debt-to-income ratio, calculated by dividing the amount of debt by the annual income. - **fico**: The FICO credit score of the borrower. - **days.with.cr.line**: The number of days the borrower has held a credit line. - **revol.bal**: The borrower's unpaid revolving balance at the end of the credit card billing cycle. - **revol.util**: The borrower’s credit utilization rate, which shows how much of the credit line is used compared to the total available credit. - **inq.last.6mths**: The number of inquiries made by creditors regarding the borrower in the last 6 months. - **delinq.2yrs**: The number of times the borrower was 30 or more days late on a payment in the past 2 years. - **pub.rec**: The borrower's number of negative public records, such as bankruptcy filings, tax liens, or judgments.
